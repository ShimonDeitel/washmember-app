Privacy Policy

Car Wash Membership is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage and is never transmitted to us or any third party. We do not collect, track, or sell any personal data. The app uses Apple's StoreKit only to process optional in-app purchases; StoreKit purchase records are handled entirely by Apple under Apple's own privacy policy.

If you delete the app, your local data is deleted with it. We have no servers and no analytics.
